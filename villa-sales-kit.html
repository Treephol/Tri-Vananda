VI — Life here, day to day
What ownership actually means
Not a stay.
Not a retreat.
A life.
The morning begins with a walk through 237 acres of botanical forest and nine lakes. Breakfast arrives from the farm. The children are in the Tweenie Club. You have a longevity assessment at the CLP Medical Pillar at noon. Dinner is at Jampa. The day required no planning. That is the point.
Land
237 acres of forest & lakes
Forest walks, wetland cycling, nine lakes, botanical garden, organic farm. Less than 15% of land developed. Nature as infrastructure, not decoration.
Health
Longevity, daily
CLP Medical, Wellbeing, Movement and Nutrition Pillars. Personalised programs. Not a scheduled activity — the operating condition of daily life.
Community
Multigenerational by design
Tweenie Club, Manorah Grand Hall, Community House, the farm. Built to hold an eight-year-old and a seventy-year-old in the same unscripted day.
Service
Farm to villa, daily
Personal concierge. Organic produce delivered from the community garden. Estate management by Trisara — the same team, the same standard.
Time
A place worth returning to
Phase One operational from Feb 2022. Health Resort and CLP Residences: Q4 2026. New Montara Wellness Brand: 2027. Built for the long term.
